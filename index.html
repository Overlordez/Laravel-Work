QUBIS

This network which allows you to protect your computers from hackers using blockchain technology. At the moment, this is the most secure network and no one has yet been able to hack it.

This technology originated in Dubai. The company "Sphere" has developed this network to protect its system. But when it progressed at the expense of AI, they began using it to protect people's computers.
