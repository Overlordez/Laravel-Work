NEW ENCRYPTION

Today, we have transferred our QUBIS technology to a new encryption called NASH. The NASH algorithm is based on the principles of modern “lightweight cryptography”, but it uses managed shifts, which, while maintaining the level of persistence, is limited to a smaller number of rounds, increasing the speed of data processing.
